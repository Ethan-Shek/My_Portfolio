About Me


Hi, I’m Ethan Shek, a third-year New Media Interactive Development student at RIT who is passionate about combining creativity, technology, and storytelling to build interactive experiences that are both fun and meaningful.

When I am not designing or coding, you can find me at the gym 💪🏻, spending time with friends 🤪, or competing on the 🐯 RIT D1 League of Legends team. I enjoy challenging myself in everything I do because growth comes from exploring new experiences and learning from them.

The Flavor Behind My Creativity

The Flavor Behind My Creativity

My passion for creativity began in the kitchen. Food has always been a way for me to express myself through endless combinations of flavors. Cooking taught me that creativity is not about following a recipe but about experimenting, adapting, and finding new ways to make something your own.

Food helped shape the person I am today. It gave me confidence, strengthened my ability to communicate, and taught me how to take feedback and turn it into growth. Just like in design, every recipe is a work in progress. Each iteration adds a new flavor, a new insight, and a new way to connect with others.

I carry that same mindset into my UX/UI and front-end work. I do not just follow the recipe, I create my own. I am always exploring new ways to bring creativity into the world of design while blending technical skill with artistic experimentation. Whether I am developing a digital interface or creating a physical prototype, my goal is to design experiences that bring people together in the same way food does.

My Design Taste

My Design Taste

I believe that good design, like good food, is about balance, emotion, and experience. It should look great, feel intuitive, and leave people with something memorable. I aim to design interfaces and visuals that don’t just function, they connect.
